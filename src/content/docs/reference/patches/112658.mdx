**Update: July 21st, 2026** \
**Version: PC: 1.126.58.1030 / Mac: 1.126.58.1230**

Key Takeaways

* Autosave and Save Reminders are now available, giving you new ways to protect your progress with customizable save options that fit your playstyle.
* Memory Boost is now available on all platforms, improving memory management to deliver smoother gameplay, better performance, and increased stability - especially during longer play sessions and in larger or older saves.
* We've continued our commitment to representing more stories by adding eight new Starter Sims and culturally inspired Create-a-Sim accessories, all available for free.
* This update also introduces the Coach Repurposed Capsule, welcomes The Sims 4 Music Den Kit, and expands Marketplace Quick Buy to PlayStation players.
* We've continued refining the gameplay experience with additional notification improvements, giving players more control over alerts while reducing unnecessary interruptions.
* Alongside these new features, this update includes fixes for several of the community's most-reported issues, plus additional improvements across Build Mode, Create-a-Sim, Gallery, gameplay, and overall game stability.
