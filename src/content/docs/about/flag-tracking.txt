Flag markers are in the header, under Sidebar. They're kept on until the next patch, unless the next patch is very close to the time it was added. 
Example:

sidebar:
  order: 50
  badge:
    text: NEW
    variant: tip

New:

  badge:
    text: NEW
    variant: tip

Updated:

  badge:
    text: UPD
    variant: note
